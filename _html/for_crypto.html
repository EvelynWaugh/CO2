For crypto miners

If you are a hashpower provider, industrial mining operation or mining pool operator ImpactScope can provide you with a detailed audit of your current and historical PoW mining carbon footprint. This is a confidential service and is free of charge. We can work with you to formulate a CO₂ footprint reduction strategy and help you realign your resources towards more sustainable hashpower distribution.

For institutional investors

Are you a manager, officer or LP in a fund with current crypto holdings or plans to purchase cryptocurrencies in the current financial year? Are you responsible for ESG reporting but you are unsure how to account for the emissions of your crypto transactions? Let ImpactScope audit the environmental impact of your institutional digital asset holdings.
