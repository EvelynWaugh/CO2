Clean cooking stoves
Fuel-efficient cooking stoves in Malawi
Project status:
Phase 1 completed, Phase 2 ongoing
Standards:
Verified Carbon Standard (VCS), Gold Standard
Annual CO₂ reduction:
97,645 tonnes
Remaining availability:
17,850 tonnes

Each stove uses 75% less wood than a traditional fire saving tonnes of CO₂ emissions. Malawi is among the world’s least developed countries and deforestation rates are extremely high. The project sparks much-needed change saving trees that absorb harmful greenhouse gases. To date stoves have been installed in more than 1,000 schools.

The project currently serves 327,000 students.All the stoves are manufactured in Malawi using locally available materials. Currently the project provides full-time employment to 42 staff, working in manufacturing, distribution, training and reporting.
